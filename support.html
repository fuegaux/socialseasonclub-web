Support

We try to answer within one business day.

Contact us

Email us directly at josenegrete0910@gmail.com. For anything urgent (account locked, payment issue, safety concern), put "URGENT" in the subject line and we'll triage it first.

Common questions

I can't log in

If you signed up with email, request a new OTP and check your spam folder. If you signed up with Apple or Google, make sure you're using the same account you originally used — HandsDown treats Apple and Google logins as separate identities. Still stuck? Email us with the email or phone on the account and we'll get you back in.

How do I cancel HandsDown Club?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → HandsDown Club → Cancel Subscription. Your Club perks stay active until the end of the current billing period, then your account reverts to the free tier. Your account itself stays intact.

How do I delete my account?

In the app, go to Profile → Settings → Account → Delete Account. We'll permanently remove your profile, RSVPs, and submitted events within 30 days. If you can't reach this screen for any reason, email us and we'll handle deletion manually.

I want a refund

HandsDown Club subscriptions are billed by Apple, so refunds go through Apple. The fastest path is reportaproblem.apple.com — sign in with your Apple ID, find the HandsDown charge, and request a refund. Most refunds are processed within 48 hours by Apple's team.

I found a bug

Email us with: what you were trying to do, what happened, what you expected, your iPhone model, and your iOS version. Screenshots or screen recordings are gold. We read every bug report.

I want to report an event or user

Use the flag icon on the event detail screen, or on the user's profile. Reports are moderated within 24 hours during business days. For anything involving a safety threat, harassment, or illegal activity, email josenegrete0910@gmail.com directly and we'll prioritize it.

I want to host events as a venue or promoter

We're talking to a small group of DFW venues and promoters about partner listings during the beta. If you run a venue or recurring event series, email josenegrete0910@gmail.com with the subject "venue partnership" and we'll set up a call.

Is HandsDown available outside DFW?

Not yet. The map, event database, and recommendations are tuned for the Dallas–Fort Worth metro right now. We'd rather be the best discovery surface in one city than a half-good one in fifty. Austin and Houston are next, likely in 2027.

Is there an Android version?

Not yet. We're iOS-only during the DFW beta. Android is on the roadmap for late 2026.
