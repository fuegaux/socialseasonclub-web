FAQ

A few honest answers.

Is it free?

Yes. Discovering events, RSVPing, and hosting one Social Meetup per month are all free. Club membership ($12.99/month) unlocks more hosting, curated drops, and Club-only events. There's no ads-supported tier — we don't sell your data.

Where is it available?

Dallas–Fort Worth, today. The map and event feed are built around DFW venues and neighborhoods. We'll expand to Austin and Houston after DFW feels dense — likely 2027.

How is Social Season Club different from Eventbrite or Partiful?

Eventbrite is a ticketing platform. Partiful is great for invitations you already have plans for. Social Season Club is a discovery surface — it tells you what's happening near you that you didn't know about, and who you know is going. Hosting is built around small social moments, not big productions.

How do you keep meetups safe?

Social Season Club is 17+ and requires a verified account. Hosts can review and approve attendees on capped events (Club perk). Every event has a report flag. We moderate reports manually within 24 hours and remove anything that violates our house rules.

How do I host an event?

Open the app, tap the "+" on the map or events tab, and fill out the basics: title, venue, time, vibe. You can drop a flyer image and Social Season Club will read the details out of it for you. Publish, share the invite link, fill the room.

Is Social Season Club on Android?

Not yet. We're iOS-only during the DFW beta. Android is on the roadmap for late 2026, after we prove the core loop on one platform.
